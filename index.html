DR KISHAN MODHA

MFDS RCS Edin 2004, BDS Lond 2002 GDC No. 80987

Kishan Modha trained at the Royal London and St Bartholomew’s School of Medicine and Dentistry and qualified in 2002. Later Kishan went back to the Royal London Hospital as a senior house officer in restorative dentistry and qualified to become a member of the Faculty of Dental Surgery with the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh. His role involved supervising and training dentists in the Emergency Dental Clinic, attending to severally medically compromised patients and carrying out advanced restorative dental treatments involving, crown and bridge, root canal treatments, veneers, tooth whitening, occlusion management and aesthetic dentistry. During his academic years Kishan won the Oral Biology prize and later the Richard Gowering prize for dental excellence – Young Dentist in South Coast Region Prize. He is a friendly and loving dentist, who loves caring for all his patient’s needs, young and elderly.

PRICING

At Only One Clinic we put the focus on you and your individual needs. Due to the highly personalised nature of our work the price of treatment depends on the complexity of each case.

Your initial consultation will assess your needs, taking into account the three foundations of our work – form, function and beauty. This will then feed into your individual treatment plan focussed on achieving the smile you’ve always desired.

We only use the highest grade bespoke materials and your treatment plan will detail the options available to you and the fees associated with each option.
